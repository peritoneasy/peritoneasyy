Impact

PeritonEasy addresses unmet needs in peritoneal dialysis, potentially reducing complications and improving quality of life for ESRD patients.

Manufacturing

The device can be manufactured using injection-molded plastics, off-the-shelf sensors, and solenoid valves, enabling scalable production.

Regulatory Pathway

The device will follow FDA Class II 510(k) submission, referencing predicate dialysis equipment with added formulation technology.

Patents

Patent landscape shows opportunities for novel formulation methods and system integration for PD customization.

Reimbursement

Reimbursement strategy includes alignment with existing PD billing codes and potential cost savings from reduced complications.
