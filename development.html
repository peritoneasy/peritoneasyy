Workflow

  1. Patients receive monthly bloodwork.
  2. Data is entered into the PeritonEasy interface.
  3. System recommends an ideal formulation; physicians can adjust as needed.
  4. Devices in hospital pharmacies or clinics generate custom dialysate bags.
  5. Sterile bags are packaged and delivered to patients for home use.

Prototype

The prototype uses refillable PVC reservoirs, solenoid valves, ultrasonic sensors, and 3D-printed components. Testing demonstrated the ability to mix accurate formulations, validated by dye experiments. Future iterations will refine scale-up for clinical use.

Design Considerations

Key design priorities included: personalizing formulations from routine lab data, ensuring clinician oversight, enabling accurate and validated dispensing, and making the system compact and modular for clinical deployment. More detailed technical schematics and component-level details are kept in the team’s internal documentation and shared selectively.
