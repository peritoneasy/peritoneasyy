Workflow

Patients receive monthly lab tests. Results are uploaded to the PeritonEasy platform where algorithms suggest personalized dialysate formulations. Physicians approve adjustments before dispensing.

Prototype

The prototype integrates refillable canisters, solenoid valves, ultrasonic sensors, and a sterile buffer system. The design demonstrates feasibility for accurate mixing and safety.

Design Inputs

Design requirements include sterility assurance, precise volumetric accuracy, patient usability, integration with clinical workflows, and regulatory compliance with ISO standards.
